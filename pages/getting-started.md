## Why Excalidraw?

Excalidraw is an open-source virtual whiteboard with a deliberately hand-drawn feel. It is fast enough for rough thinking and flexible enough for diagrams you want to share.

## Your first canvas

- Open [excalidraw.com](https://excalidraw.com).
- Choose a shape from the toolbar or press a number key from `1` to `9`.
- Drag on the canvas, then double-click a shape to add text.
- Use the selection tool to move, resize, group, or duplicate objects.

## Save and share

Your work is stored locally by default. Export a portable `.excalidraw` file if you want to keep editing later, or export PNG/SVG for sharing. For live collaboration, create a shareable room and remember that its encryption key lives in the URL.

> Treat collaboration links like private documents: anyone with the full link may be able to enter the room.
