---
title: Better collaborative sessions
---

## Before the room opens

Prepare a small starter canvas. Add a clear title, one sentence of instructions, and a visible area for each activity. Lock background elements so participants cannot move the structure accidentally.

## During the session

- Begin with a low-stakes warm-up so everyone learns the controls.
- Give participants a parking-lot area for thoughts that do not fit yet.
- Use frames and consistent colors to make the canvas scannable.
- Time-box silent work before opening the group discussion.

## Finish deliberately

End by circling decisions, naming an owner for each action, and exporting a snapshot. A beautiful canvas is less valuable than a clear record of what happens next.
