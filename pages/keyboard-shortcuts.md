title: Keyboard shortcuts worth learning
---

## The essential set

- `V` or `1` — selection tool
- `R` or `2` — rectangle
- `D` or `4` — draw freely
- `T` or `8` — text
- `Cmd/Ctrl + D` — duplicate selection
- `Cmd/Ctrl + G` — group selection
- `Cmd/Ctrl + Shift + G` — ungroup
- `Cmd/Ctrl + K` — attach a link
- `Shift + 1` — zoom to fit all elements

## A useful rhythm

Keep one hand on the keyboard. Select a tool with its shortcut, draw, then press `V` to return to selection. Hold `Shift` while resizing to preserve proportions and hold `Alt/Option` to resize from the center.
